# Open Research Europe Operating Manual

Welcome to the Open Research Europe Operating Manual!
This is a customized supplement to the generic OJS documentation that covers the journey of a submitted manuscript to publication following ORE's operating procedure.

For foundational information about OJS account management and basic journal configuration, please review the existing documentation.
* [About OJS: Registering & Managing Your Account](https://docs.pkp.sfu.ca/learning-ojs/about-ojs/en/#basics)
* [Learning OJS 3.5 for Journal Managers](https://docs.pkp.sfu.ca/learning-ojs/journal-managers/en/)

Once you have been assigned the Editor role, you are ready to evaluate and manage submissions. We recommend reading each chapter in order to start, and then returning to chapters related to each stage of the editorial workflow as needed.
